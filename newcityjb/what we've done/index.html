The "AgentZ" Layer

AgentZ was not deployed as a chatbot, but as a high-order navigation layer. It digested complex Council DCPs (Development Control Plans) and cross-referenced them with real-world Industry pain points to isolate friction areas like S.138 driveways and Ridge Height ambiguity.

⚡

Permissionless Infrastructure

We built frameworks that did not require permission to exist. By establishing protocols that functioned outside the slow internal machinery but aligned with its rules, we created tools that could be adopted immediately, rather than waiting for a committee vote.

⚙

Critical Decision Support

The framework didn't just automate tasks; it refined protocols at critical decision points. It flagged when a "human in the loop" was necessary versus when an automated "S7.11 Estimate" could safely trigger, ensuring speed without losing governance.
