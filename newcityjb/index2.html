The Cost of Caution

The US memo mandates a radical reduction in "Authority to Operate" (ATO) timelines, effectively creating a 30-day fast lane. In contrast, standard Australian Local Government procurement involving rigorous manual risk assessments often spans 6-12 months.

Key Insight: Reciprocity

The "Department of War" strategy forces agencies to accept the security accreditations of others.

For Newcastle: Why re-assess a tool that NSW Government or a Federal agency has already cleared? Adopting a "Presumption of Approval" based on higher-tier accreditation is the single biggest efficiency lever available.

Strategic Pivot for Newcastle

Moving from "Ad-Hoc Adoption" to an "AI-First Council." This requires borrowing the structural rigor of the US Military model while maintaining the community focus of Australian governance.

3. Re-imagine, Don't Bolt-on

The Shift: Shadow to Strategic

Currently, much AI use in councils is "Shadow AI" (unauthorized ChatGPT use for drafting). The goal is to move this activity into "Official Systems" and high-value "Data Analysis" (e.g., traffic patterns, waste management).
