Rebuilding Trust: Advice & Communication

Trust is the primary currency of pandemic management. To fix the gaps, the government must radically change where it gets advice and how it speaks.

1. Inputs: Who should the government listen to?

The Failure (2020): Advice was insular, heavily weighted towards political strategists and security agencies, with health advice often kept secret.

The Fix (2026): Trust requires an "Open Architecture". We must elevate community leaders and independent scientists to the same level as federal bureaucrats.

Key Insight: "You cannot communicate effectively with a community you do not listen to."
